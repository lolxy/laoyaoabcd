Company Overview

Orion Group of Companies, entity of Orion Industry and Trade Co. Ltd, is the leading international clothes, shoes, food and constructions material company in China. Orion Industry and Trade Co. Our goals are to providehigh quality and innovative products to our customers around the world and generate superior returns for shareholders while operating our industrial and trade business sustainably and with integrity. All our companies are wholly owned subsidiary of Orion Industry and Trade Co.
